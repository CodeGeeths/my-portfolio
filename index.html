My introduction

I am an Infrastructure Engineer with over 4 years of experience designing, building, and scaling reliable systems. My work focuses on developing robust observability platforms and automation frameworks that enable efficient monitoring, faster incident resolution, and improved system reliability at scale.

My technical expertise spans modern cloud-native and distributed systems, including AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, Jenkins, and Kafka. I have hands-on experience with automation using Ansible and Python, and bring expert-level proficiency in designing and maintaining reusable CI/CD pipelines that enable seamless and scalable software delivery.

My interests lie at the intersection of Cloud, DevOps, Infrastructure, SRE, and AIOps, with a strong focus on building systems that improve developer productivity and operational efficiency.

In addition, I am actively exploring the integration of AI into infrastructure and operations. I have experience working with Amazon Bedrock models and am interested in leveraging AI to enhance observability, automate debugging, and build intelligent systems for next-generation infrastructure platforms.
