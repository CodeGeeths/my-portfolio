About Me

My introduction

I'm a Software Engineer with over 4 years of experience building scalable, observable, and fault-tolerant distributed systems. My work centers on designing resilient infrastructure and observability platforms that reduce operational toil and improve reliability at scale.

My technical expertise spans Kubernetes, OpenShift, AWS, Docker, Terraform, and Helm, paired with observability tooling like OpenTelemetry, Grafana, Loki, and Prometheus. I build reliable CI/CD and GitOps workflows with GitLab CI/CD, ArgoCD, Jenkins, and Ansible.

I bring an AI-first mindset to infrastructure work, building AI-powered tooling such as troubleshooting agents that search cross-service logs to pinpoint root cause, cutting mean-time-to-resolution and reducing the operational burden on engineering teams.

My interests sit at the intersection of Distributed Systems, SRE, and AIOps, with a strong focus on building systems that make engineering teams faster and more confident in production.
